Identifying what type of skylights you have

This section will help decide on what type of skylight you have.

Is your skylight glass or arylic?

Arylic Skylight

Arylic skylights will consist of a plastic top that can either be a bronze/clear arylic or white/clear arylic top. It will have a curve to it and possibly will be an older skylight. There are two types of arylic skylights which is curb-mounted or self-flashed. Please click the button below if you are unsure of what you have.

Scroll to the arylic skylight section

Glass Skylight

Glass skylights will have glass instead of arylic and will be flat. There are three types of glass skylights. If you are unsure of what type of skylight you have click below.
